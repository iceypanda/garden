Cleaning

Cleaning with bleach

  1. Spray bleach (Cillit bang) onto sponge in bucket then apply
  2. Leave bleach for 5-10 mins
  3. Rinse with warm water

Curtains

  1. Remove hooks or will come off in washing machine
  2. Spray mould patches with bleach on both sides of lining
  3. Leave bleach for 10-15 mins
  4. Rinse whole curtain with warm water
  5. Wash curtains at 30°C on handwash or synthetic for longer wash
